About Us

Safe Haven is a non-profit organization that is involved in the overall empowerment of the girl child. We help to equip young girls with the values and vision to pursue their dreams. Our goal is to inspire black, brown, and other underserved women and family members to break through barriers, take control of their own futures, and seize opportunities for good through financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and technology training.

We do this by providing scholarships for secondary education, providing extracurricular training and providing internships. We also help to support child brides and young mothers with skills needed to survive. Additionally, we find it equally important to support health initiatives and offer disaster relief in times of need. Safe Haven is building the next generation of black and brown youth as diverse entrepreneurs and college educated leaders in their communities through inclusive capitalism and positive mindset training.
